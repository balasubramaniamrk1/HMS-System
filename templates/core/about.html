Our Story

Founded in 2005, HealthPlus Multi-Speciality Hospital has grown from a 50-bed nursing home to a 500-bed quaternary care facility. We are dedicated to providing world-class healthcare at affordable costs.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic equipment, manned by a team of internationally trained doctors and dedicated support staff.
